About me

Front-end developer from Dublin, Ireland

Hi! I'm currently studying front end web development at SheCodes. I have been involved in projects with various roles such as web design, digital marketing and UX / UI. Lately, I have discovered my passion for coding and I'm looking forward to work on projects as a front end developer.
